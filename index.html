ABOUT


Siti Nurul Maryam Abdullah is a senior lecturer born in Kuala Lumpur and she has been working under
qualification Information Managementin UiTM for 11 years. Currently she beholding a permanent position
as senior lecturer under Faculty of Information Management, Universiti Teknologi MARA. Her teaching and
research interests are in Human Relations in Resource Center, Cooperative Learning and Smart Education
for Resource Center Management.
